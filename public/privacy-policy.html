Application Privacy Policy for ryzomatic.net

Last updated: November 2, 2025

Welcome to ryzomatic.net ("we," "us," or "our"). ryzomatic.net is owned and operated by VStyle.ca. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our cloud-native intelligent reading platform (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

a) Account Information

When you create an account, we use Supabase for authentication. We may collect your email address and a securely hashed password or information from a third-party login provider (e.g., Google, GitHub).

b) User-Uploaded Content (Documents)

You may upload documents (such as PDFs and text files) to the Service. Your documents are securely uploaded to and stored on Amazon Web Services (AWS) S3. This allows you to access your content across multiple devices.

c) Application & Metadata

To provide a seamless, synced experience, we store application data in our Supabase database. This includes:

  • Your user preferences (e.g., theme, font size).
  • Document metadata (e.g., file names, page count).
  • Your highlights and annotations.

d) AI & Text-to-Speech (TTS) Feature Data

Our Service uses Google Cloud Platform (GCP) to provide advanced features.

  • AI Chat: When you use the AI Chat feature, the relevant text content from your document is sent to Google's Gemini AI API to generate a response.
  • Text-to-Speech: When you use the TTS feature, the text you select is sent to GCP Text-to-Speech to generate audio.

We do not store the content of your AI requests or TTS audio files after the request is completed, other than temporary caching for performance.

e) User-Provided API Keys

To enable the AI and TTS features, the Service may require you to provide your own API keys for Google Cloud Platform. These keys are securely stored and encrypted in our Supabase database. They are used only by our backend services to make API calls to GCP on your behalf and are not visible to us in plain text.

f) Anonymous Usage Data

We may collect standard, anonymous server logs when you access the Service. This may include your IP address, browser type, and access times, as well as logs from our service providers (AWS, Supabase, and GCP). This data is used only for monitoring the Service's health, debugging, and ensuring security.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide and Sync the Service: To manage your account, store and retrieve your documents from AWS S3, and sync your highlights and preferences from Supabase.
  • To Process AI and TTS Requests: To securely send your content to GCP to provide the AI and TTS features.
  • To Improve User Experience: To remember your settings and preferences.
  • To Maintain the Service: To monitor for errors, secure the platform, and analyze anonymous usage statistics.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade any of your personal information or document content. Your data is shared only with the following third-party service providers (sub-processors) who are necessary to operate the Service:

  • Supabase: Provides our database, user authentication, and backend services. Your account info, metadata, and encrypted API keys are stored with Supabase.
  • Amazon Web Services (AWS): Provides S3 cloud storage for your uploaded documents.
  • Google Cloud Platform (GCP): Provides the AI (Gemini) and Text-to-Speech (TTS) functionalities.

We may also disclose your information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our company or others.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. This includes:

  • Encryption-in-Transit: Using HTTPS (SSL) for all data transferred between your browser and our services.
  • Encryption-at-Rest: Encrypting your data stored in AWS S3 and our Supabase database.
  • Key Security: Securely encrypting your API keys in our database.

You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account password.

5. Data Retention

We retain your User Content (documents in AWS S3) and Application Data (in Supabase) for as long as your account is active. You can delete your documents or your entire account from within the Service. Deleting your account will permanently remove your data from our systems within a reasonable time frame.

6. Children's Privacy

This Service is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
