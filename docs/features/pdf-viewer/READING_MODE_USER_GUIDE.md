## FAQ

**Q: Will my settings affect the PDF canvas view?**
A: No! Reading mode and canvas view are completely separate. Your reading mode preferences only apply to reading mode.

**Q: Can I use TTS in reading mode?**
A: Yes! TTS works perfectly in reading mode with word highlighting that adapts to your chosen theme.

**Q: Do my settings save?**
A: Yes! All typography and theme preferences are saved automatically and persist across sessions.

**Q: Can I adjust settings while reading?**
A: Absolutely! Click the Type icon in the header to open settings without exiting reading mode.

**Q: What's the best theme for long reading?**
A: Sepia is generally most comfortable for extended sessions, but personal preference varies. Try all three!
